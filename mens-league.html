Men's League

Men's League on Wednesday Nights

League will begin on April 9 and run through August 27 in 2025. Optional proximity contest each week, and the formats change each week.

Cost is $10 for members and $15 for non members. Cost includes, 9 holes, cart, and chance to win cash prizes.

Entry fee will be broken down as a $7pp goes tournament payout for the night, $2pp goes to skins payout for the night, and $1pp goes into a pot for a drawing at the end of the season. Each week you participate gets you 1 entry into the pot of gold at the end of the year. The more you play the better chances you have to win part of the Pot of Gold. All dollars that are put into the Pot of Gold will be broken down with 3 winners drawn and paying 50%, 30%, and 20% of the pot.

All are welcome to come and play in our league each week.

Schedule below.

2025 Men’s League Schedule

We will tee off at 6pm each week Wednesday evening. Check in begins at 5:30. $10 for members and $15 for non-members.

April 9th - 4 Man Scramble

April 16th - Odds and Evens - 4 players break into two scramble teams. High score odds / low score evens.

April 23rd - 3 Man Scramble

April 30th - 2 Man Scramble

May 7th - 4 Man Scramble / Shamble - Scramble even holes/shamble odd holes.

May 14th - HELL NIGHT 4 Man Scramble

May 21st - 4 Man Wildcard (3 Scramble /1 Shamble) - Shamble player rotates. Add both scores.

May 28th - Carts/Riders/Opposites - 2 two-man scramble switch partners every 3 holes.

June 4th - 3 Man Scramble - 1 person putts each hole and must alternate.

June 11th - 4 Man 4 Club Scramble - Any four clubs you want for each person.

June 18th - 4 Man Shamble - Take 3 scores.

June 25th - 2 Man Scramble – 1 player putts odd holes, 1 player putts even holes.

July 2nd - 4 Scramble - Black tees par 5’s / green tees par 4’s / yellow tees par 3’s.

July 9th - 4 Man Wildcard (3 scramble, 1 shamble) - Shamble player rotates and add scores together.

July 16th - 2 Man Scramble / Shamble - Scramble odd holes / shamble even holes.

July 23rd - 4 Man Shamble / Scramble - Team scramble first two shots / shamble from there take 3 scores.

July 30th - Carts/Riders/Opposites - 2 two-man scramble partners switch partners every 3 holes take 1 score.

August 6th - 3 Man Scramble

August 13th - HELL NIGHT 4 Man Scramble

August 20th - 2 Man Scramble

August 27th - Short Course 4 Man Scramble
