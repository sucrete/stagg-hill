Ladies Evening League

Ladies Evening League on Thursday Nights

League will run from April 24 - August 28, 2025.

This league is designed to allow the working ladies, stay at home moms, and any other ladies that would like to come out and have on Thursday evenings. Golf will begin at 6pm and sign up starts at 5:00pm. Feel free to call ahead to sign up if you will be running close to our start time. (785) 539-1041

Cost is $10 for members and $15 for non members. Cost includes, 9 holes, cart, and chance to win cash prizes. $1 of each entry will be held back each week for an end of the season meal. We also offer optional proximity contests for $1 per contest each week, which pays out cash.

All play will be from the forward Yellow Tees unless otherwise specified. This league night is designed to get ladies on the golf course on a regular basis and have some fun by playing formats that are low stress.

All ladies are welcome to come and play in our league each week.
