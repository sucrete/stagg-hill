Couples League on Friday Nights

2025 Couples League is OPEN to any couples that would like to join us for some Friday night golf. Cost is $10 per couple for members and $20 per couple for non-members and includes 9 holes with cart and prize money. $2 of each entry will be set aside for an end of the year meal. You can also sign up each week for proxi prizes which are optional. Proxi contests are $1 per couple for each proxi contest, and it pays back cash.

Check in is at 5:00pm and we shotgun at 6:00pm each Friday.

Hope to see you out each week!
