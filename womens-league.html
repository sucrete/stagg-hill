Women's League

Stagg Hill Ladies Golf Association, 2025

The Stagg Hill Ladies Golf Association is excited to begin another season of golf! We welcome golfers of all skill levels who have a passion for the game and want to improve their skills. The Tuesday morning league runs from April 1st through October 28th. Each week golfers compete in fun games where all abilities have a chance to win cash prizes. *A GHIN handicap is required for some games, but not all! Yearly awards are also given at the end-of-season banquet. Please join us on March 25th at 10 am for our kick-off brunch at Stagg Hill.

For further information check our website: SHLGA Websiteor contact Kim Crusinberry: crusinberryfam@att.net (785) 565-3107 We look forward to a great year on and off the course!
