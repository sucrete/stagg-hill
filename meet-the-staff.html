Meet The Staff

Chad Myers, PGA

General Manager & Director of Golf
(785) 539-1041 chad@stagghillgolfclub.com
- Kansas State University '96 -
Chad is a Member of the PGA of America since 2001 who grew up in Manhattan playing golf at Stagg Hill. Chad played golf for Manhattan High School (6A Team Champs '91), Garden City C.C., Cloud County C.C. where he quailified for NJCAA Div.II Nationals twice, and then walked on to play at Kansas State University. Chad is a member of the Midwest Section PGA where he has served on the Board of Directors for over 16 years including serving as the President for two years. Teaching the game is a passion for Chad who has taught for over 20 years. He has taught juniors, beginners, high school players (many of which went on to play collegiately) and some college players. Chad is married to his wife Kaylee and they have two daughters both of whom play and enjoy the great game of golf!

Join Our Team

Employment Application

Completed Applications can be emailed to info@stagghillgolfclub.com or brought to Stagg Hill G.C. 4441 Stagg Hill Rd. Manhattan, KS. 66502

If you are interested in joining the team at Stagg Hill Golf Club we would love to hear from you. We are looking for people who want to work, have great personalities, and enjoy people. Positions that we hire for include Golf Course Maintenance Staff, Outside Services, Beverage Cart, and Clubhouse Staff. See below for duties for each position.

We are currently hiring Golf Course Maintenance, Outside Services, & Beverage Cart. We are looking for employees available through the summer and into the fall.

Golf Course Maintenance Staff duties include mowing, weed eating, course clean up, course prep, etc.

Outside Service Staff duties include golf cart set up, cart cleaning, trash duties, range picking, tournament set up, etc.

Clubhouse Staff duties include pro shop and snack bar sales, ringing in green fees, taking tee times, utilizing POS System, restocking, cleaning, opening and closing duties, tournament set up and break down, beverage cart service, etc.

Benefits for all positions include competative hourly wages, golf privileges, range privileges, and cart privileges, food and beverage discounts, & pro shop discounts.
